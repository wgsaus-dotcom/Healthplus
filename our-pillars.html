employment risk.

Every worker placed by Health Plus International is employed by us — not you. That means payroll, super, workers compensation, AHPRA verification, police clearances, and NSW Health credentialling are our responsibility.

You receive one weekly all-inclusive invoice. You manage the clinical direction. We handle everything else.
