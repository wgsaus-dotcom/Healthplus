WHAT WE PLACE

Nursing & allied health for Far West NSW

Every practitioner is verified and vetted per NSW Health and Department of Health and Ageing guidelines, pre-screened, and placed under a Health Plus International employment contract — you manage clinical operations, we manage everything else.

🏥
CRITICAL NEED

Registered Nurses — General & Emergency

Far West NSW hospitals face consistent ER and ward nursing shortfalls. We place RNs with documented remote area experience, available for short-term relief through to extended rotational arrangements.

🧠
CRITICAL NEED

Psychologists & Mental Health Nurses

Mental health service gaps across Far West NSW are acute. We place credentialled psychologists and credentialled mental health nurses for both clinical and community settings.

🦶
HIGH NEED

Physiotherapists & Occupational Therapists

Chronic disease burden in remote communities drives high demand for physio and OT. We place practitioners experienced in both acute and community settings, including aged care.

🚑
HIGH NEED

Paramedics & Remote Area Nurses

Extended care paramedicine and RANs are essential across isolated communities. We place practitioners with ACRRM or similar remote-area endorsement where required.

🦷
HIGH NEED

Dental Practitioners

Access to dental care in Far West NSW is severely limited. We place dentists and dental therapists for outreach and fixed-term community dental programs.

🤝
HIGH NEED

Aboriginal Health Practitioners

Culturally informed care is central to health outcomes in Far West NSW communities. We place qualified Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Health Practitioners with experience in remote settings.

Health Plus International is the employer. Not your facility.

Every practitioner placed in Far West NSW is employed by Health Plus International under a Fair Work compliant contract. Your facility receives a credentialled clinician on day one — without taking on a single employment obligation.

  • Registration verified per NSW Health and DoH guidelines
  • National Police Check and WWC included
  • Workers' compensation and PI insurance held by us
  • Payroll, superannuation, and leave managed by us
  • One all-inclusive weekly invoice — no surprises
