Our Story

Built for the
communities
others forgot.

Health Plus International was founded on a single conviction — that regional and remote NSW communities deserve the same quality healthcare access as any city.

Why We Exist

The workforce exists.
The need is real.
We are the bridge.

Regional and remote communities across NSW face a critical shortage of healthcare workers. Not because the workers don't exist — they do. Not because communities don't need care — they desperately do.

The gap is infrastructure. Health Plus International is that infrastructure. We handle employment, compliance, credentialling, and logistics — so communities get care and workers get purpose.

Our Model

How we're different.

Employer of Record

We employ every healthcare worker we place. Payroll, super, insurance, and compliance sit with us — not you. One invoice. Zero HR headaches.

Regional Focus

We don't do metro placements. Every worker we place goes to a regional or remote NSW community. That's not a niche — that's our mission.

Compliance First

AHPRA verified. Police checked. Credentialled to NSW Health standards. Every candidate, every time — before they set foot in your facility.
