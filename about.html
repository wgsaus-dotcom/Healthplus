Our Story

Built from the ground up
for regional Australia.

HealthPlus International was founded in 2026 with a clear-eyed understanding of a problem that had existed for decades — regional and remote NSW communities were chronically under-resourced with healthcare workers, and the organisations designed to solve it weren't built for it.

Most workforce providers operate from cities, for cities. Regional placements are treated as difficult, expensive afterthoughts. Communities in Broken Hill, Walgett, Bourke, and Lightning Ridge were left to navigate a system that wasn't designed with them in mind.

We built HealthPlus International specifically to serve these communities. Not as a side offering. Not as a niche add-on. As our entire purpose.

We are a labour hire employer of record — which means we employ every healthcare worker we place, carry all employment obligations, and give our clients a single all-inclusive invoice. No HR complexity. No compliance risk. Just the right person, in the right community, ready to contribute.

THE PROBLEM WE SET OUT TO SOLVE

Regional and remote NSW communities have a healthcare workforce challenge that metro-focused providers can't solve. The workers are out there. The communities need them. What's missing is the connection — and the infrastructure to make it work at scale.

That's exactly what HealthPlus International was built to provide.

Our Motto

Three words.
Everything we stand for.

People. Care. Compliance. is not a marketing slogan. It is the operating principle behind every decision we make, every worker we place, and every community we serve. Each word carries real weight.

P
People.

The word comes first because it is what this entire business is built around.


The healthcare workers who leave their families, travel far from home, and step into unfamiliar communities to deliver care — they deserve to be treated as professionals. Fair pay, proper support, clear expectations, and an employer who genuinely has their back.


The community members in Broken Hill, Walgett, Bourke, and Lightning Ridge who depend on healthcare workers for daily support, aged care, disability services, and basic health needs — they deserve the same quality of care as anyone in a major city.


Every placement decision we make starts with people. Not metrics. Not margins. People.

C
Care.

Care is both what we deliver and how we operate.


What we deliver is direct — we place healthcare workers in communities that carry disproportionate health burdens. High rates of chronic disease, ageing populations, disability needs, and mental health challenges that go unmet not because the community doesn't deserve support, but because the workforce hasn't been there.


How we operate reflects the same value. We take care in every placement. We brief every worker before they arrive. We support them through the engagement. We respond to facility managers within 24 hours because when a community is short-staffed, time matters.


Care isn't a value we display on a wall. It is the standard we hold every placement to.

C
Compliance.

In healthcare and community services, compliance is not a checkbox. It is the foundation of trust.


For the facilities and services we work with — every worker we place is credentialled, police checked, and has confirmed working rights before they set foot in your organisation. You receive a full compliance summary. You take on zero employment obligation. One invoice. We carry it all.


For the workers we employ — Fair Work compliance, correct Award rates, superannuation, workers' compensation, and professional indemnity are managed by us. They focus on the work. We handle the rest.


Compliance is how we protect the communities, the workers, and the organisations we serve. It comes last in our motto, but it runs through everything we do.

What Makes Us Different

Built for regional Australia.
Not adapted for it.

Every design decision in how HealthPlus International operates — from how we source workers to how we manage compliance to how we price our services — reflects the specific realities of regional and remote healthcare placement.

🎯

Regional-first, not regional-adapted

We were built from day one to serve regional and remote NSW communities. This isn't a niche offering alongside metro operations — it is our entire focus. That means we understand the terrain, the LHDs, the towns, and the challenges in a way that general providers do not.

🤝

We are the employer — full stop

As a labour hire employer of record, we employ every worker we place. Your facility or service takes on zero employment obligation. No contracts to manage, no payroll to run, no compliance exposure. One all-inclusive invoice. We carry the rest.

✅

Credentialled before they arrive

Every healthcare worker we place is fully screened before any offer is made — credentials verified, National Police Check completed, working rights confirmed. You receive a compliance summary. There are no surprises on day one.

🌏

An international pipeline that's genuinely motivated

We source healthcare workers from the Philippines and India who are actively motivated to live and work in regional Australia — not placed reluctantly in a remote posting. Motivation matters for continuity, quality of care, and community integration.

⚡

24-hour response for urgent needs

When a regional or remote facility is short-staffed, the impact is immediate and real. We respond to all workforce requests within 24 hours — and for urgent community needs, typically sooner. Speed is part of the service.

📋

Transparent, all-inclusive pricing

Regional placements involve real additional costs — travel, accommodation, remote loadings. We build everything into a single rate. No hidden charges. No surprise invoices. Your facility knows exactly what it costs before the engagement begins.

Abhay J Kumar founded HealthPlus International with a specific conviction — that the healthcare workforce challenge in regional and remote NSW was a solvable problem, and that solving it required an organisation built specifically for it, not one trying to serve metro and regional markets simultaneously.

With a background spanning registered training organisations, labour hire, international business development, and healthcare workforce management, Abhay brings a practical understanding of both the regulatory environment and the operational realities of placing workers in complex, remote settings.

His approach to HealthPlus International is grounded in three principles that map directly to the company's motto: put people first in every decision, deliver care to communities that have been underserved for too long, and operate with a standard of compliance that gives both clients and workers complete confidence.

HealthPlus International is based in Sydney and serves communities across regional and remote New South Wales — from the Far West LHD to the Mid North Coast, from Broken Hill to Tamworth to Kempsey.
