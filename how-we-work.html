The Process

Four steps to
care on the ground.

01

Submit a Request

Tell us the role type, location, start date, and duration. Online form or direct call — we respond within 24 hours. No exceptions.

02

We Match & Verify

We identify a suitable candidate from our network. AHPRA verified, police checked, credentialled to NSW Health standards before any placement.

03

We Are the Employer

We employ the worker directly. We handle contracts, payroll, super, workers comp, and insurance. You receive one all-inclusive weekly invoice.

04

Care Delivered

The worker arrives on site. You manage clinical direction. We remain the employment support contact for the duration of the placement.

The EOR Model

What employer
of record means.

When we say we're the employer of record, we mean it literally. Every healthcare worker placed through Health Plus International is on our books — not yours.

This means you get the workforce you need without taking on the employment obligations, insurance complexity, or HR administration that comes with it.

What we handle

Employment contract · Payroll & super · Workers compensation · Public liability · AHPRA verification · Police clearance · Credentialling · HR support · Timesheet management

What you handle

Clinical direction · Rostering · Day-to-day supervision · One weekly invoice

Credentialling

Verified before

AHPRA Verified

All registered health practitioners are verified against the AHPRA register before placement. Current, active, and unrestricted registration confirmed.

Police Checked

National Police Clearance for every worker. Renewed in line with NSW Health requirements.

NSW Health Standards

All workers are credentialled to NSW Health and Department of Health requirements — immunisation, identification, certifications, and role-specific requirements.
