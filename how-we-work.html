COMMON QUESTIONS

Questions About Our Model

How the employer of record model works in practice.

As the employer of record, HealthPlus International employs every healthcare worker we place. We handle all employment obligations — payroll, superannuation, workers compensation, Fair Work compliance, and professional indemnity insurance. The facility manages clinical direction on site; we manage everything else.
HealthPlus International handles: credential verification, AHPRA checks, National Police Clearances, employment contracts, payroll, superannuation, workers compensation, professional indemnity insurance, and ongoing compliance. The facility handles: rostering, clinical supervision, and day-to-day direction of the worker on site.
One all-inclusive invoice. All employment costs — wages, superannuation, workers compensation, insurance — are wrapped into a single weekly invoice to the facility. No hidden charges, no separate agency fees, no surprises.
We respond to all requests within 24 hours. Placement timelines depend on role, location, and urgency. For critical shortages in Far West and Western NSW we prioritise immediately. Most placements are confirmed within 3–10 business days of the initial request.
All workers hold verified qualifications, a current National Police Clearance, and where applicable, current AHPRA registration. We verify all credentials against original documents before deployment and maintain compliance records for the duration of each placement.

HealthPlus International strategically places healthcare workers, AINs, support workers and allied health professionals in regional and remote NSW communities. We are the employer of record.
