FROM ENQUIRY TO PLACEMENT

How a placement works

Four steps from your initial request to a credentialled clinician on the floor.

1
SAME DAY

Submit a Workforce Request

Tell us the role, discipline, location, and roster dates. We respond within one business day — typically the same day for urgent requests.

2
2–5 DAYS

We Source & Credential

We identify credentialled practitioners with documented rural experience. All pre-placement checks are completed before we present a candidate profile.

3
YOUR APPROVAL

You Confirm the Placement

Review the practitioner profile and approve. We manage the employment contract, logistics, and any travel arrangements from that point forward.

4
ONGOING

We Stay the Employer

You manage clinical operations. We handle payroll, compliance, and practitioner support for the full duration of the engagement — one invoice, no surprises.
