OUR COMMITMENT TO YOUR FACILITY

We care for your operations.
You focus on the care.

Aged care facilities in regional NSW face workforce challenges that metro services do not. We understand the immediacy of your needs, and we have the experience, knowledge, and systems to respond quickly, effectively, and in a highly confidential manner.

01
We understand your requirements first
We take the time to understand your facility, your resident population, your roster structure, and your team culture — not just the role to be filled. A care worker who is the right match clinically and culturally makes a genuine difference to your residents.
02
We source from domestic and international markets
We draw from both the Australian domestic workforce and our international sourcing pipeline — Philippines, India, UK, and Ireland — giving you access to a candidate pool that no single regional facility can build on its own. We receive tens of thousands of applications monthly across our network.
03
We recommend only qualified, verified candidates
Every candidate we recommend holds verified qualifications, a current National Police Clearance, and where applicable, an NDIS Worker Screening Check. We evaluate each candidate against your specific requirements — we never send you a resume without being confident it is a genuine match.
04
We maximise your productivity outcomes
We assist you in maximising workforce productivity by identifying candidates who match your requirements precisely — not just someone available. We recommend workforce management approaches to simplify your staffing function and reduce the variable costs of last-minute recruitment.
05
We manage every employment obligation
As the employer of record, we handle all payroll, superannuation, workers compensation, Fair Work compliance, and professional indemnity insurance. One all-inclusive invoice. No hidden charges. Your facility manages clinical direction — we manage everything else.
06
We pursue a long-term partnership
Our goal is to become the preferred workforce partner of every aged care facility we work with — not a transactional supplier. We pursue everlasting relationships by providing services that simplify your staffing management, reduce costs, and ensure your residents always have the care they need.

COMPLIANCE & VERIFICATION

Every worker. Fully verified.
Before their first shift.

Regional aged care facilities cannot afford a compliance failure. Every worker we place is verified against the full requirement set for their role and setting before they arrive at your door.

All aged care & support workers

  • ✓Verified qualifications — original documents sighted
  • ✓National Police Clearance — current, no older than 3 years
  • ✓Employment references — minimum two verified
  • ✓Right to work in Australia confirmed
  • ✓First Aid and CPR — current where required by role
  • ✓Manual handling training — verified for care settings
  • ✓Infection control — verified to current standards

NDIS support workers — additional requirements

  • ✓NDIS Worker Screening Check — current and active
  • ✓NDIS Worker Orientation Module — completed
  • ✓Understanding of NDIS Code of Conduct
  • ✓Restrictive practices awareness — where applicable
  • ✓Incident reporting obligations understood
  • ✓Supported Decision Making principles understood
  • ✓Cultural safety training — verified for Aboriginal communities

All credentials verified to NSW Health and Department of Health standards. We maintain compliance records for every worker for the duration of each placement and notify facilities of any upcoming expiry. We are the employer — your facility does not carry the compliance burden.

REGIONS WE COVER

Six regional LHDs.
Every aged care setting.

We place aged care and support workers across all six regional Local Health Districts in NSW — prioritising the communities with the most critical workforce shortages.

CRITICAL SHORTAGE

Far West NSW LHD

The most acute aged care workforce shortage in NSW. Single-facility towns with no backup workforce, high Aboriginal population requiring culturally safe care workers.

Broken Hill · Wilcannia · Bourke · Wentworth · Ivanhoe
CRITICAL SHORTAGE

Western NSW LHD

Large geographic footprint with dispersed aged care facilities across dozens of small towns. Aged care workers are among the most requested workforce type in this LHD.

Orange · Dubbo · Bathurst · Parkes · Cowra · Walgett
HIGH DEMAND

Murrumbidgee LHD

Growing aged population across the Riverina with strong demand for residential care and NDIS support workers, particularly in smaller communities outside Wagga Wagga.

Wagga Wagga · Griffith · Albury · Deniliquin · Young
HIGH DEMAND

Hunter New England LHD

Significant demand for aged care and support workers across rural communities in the Hunter and New England regions, particularly north and northwest of Newcastle.

Tamworth · Armidale · Moree · Gunnedah · Inverell
MODERATE DEMAND

Northern NSW LHD

Coastal and hinterland communities with growing aged populations and demand for community care workers and NDIS support staff, particularly in more isolated townships.

Lismore · Tweed Heads · Casino · Kyogle · Mullumbimby
MODERATE DEMAND

Mid North Coast LHD

Popular retirement destination with above-average aged care demand and ongoing need for community care and support workers across coastal and inland communities.

Port Macquarie · Coffs Harbour · Taree · Kempsey · Bellingen

Stage 01
Identification & assessment
We identify candidates in source countries whose qualifications, experience, and character profile match the needs of regional aged care. We assess at source — before any visa or registration process begins.
Stage 02
Skills assessment & registration
We guide each worker through Australian skills assessment, qualification recognition, and the relevant registration or screening checks for their role — NDIS Worker Screening, police clearance, and occupation-specific requirements.
Stage 03
Placement matching
We match credentialled international workers to specific regional facilities based on role requirements, care culture, and community fit. We do not place speculatively — every placement is deliberate and matched.
Stage 04
Arrival, induction & settlement
We support workers through arrival, accommodation, community orientation, and workplace induction. We remain engaged throughout the initial placement period to ensure both the worker and the facility are fully supported.
