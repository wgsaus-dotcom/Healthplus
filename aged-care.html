The Problem We Solve

Aged care in
regional NSW is
in crisis.

Regional aged care facilities face a compounding crisis — an ageing population growing faster than the workforce available to care for it.

Staff turnover is high. Recruitment is difficult. And the workers who do want to work in regional aged care often can't navigate the logistics of getting there.

We solve the logistics. We source the workers, handle the employment, manage the compliance — and deliver credentialled aged care staff to regional facilities that need them.
