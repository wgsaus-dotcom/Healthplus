Compliance managed

In remote settings,
compliance isn't optional.

Remote facilities carry greater risk when something goes wrong and the nearest backup is hours away. Every worker we place undergoes a mandatory pre-deployment check — and you receive a full compliance summary before their first shift.

Verification & Vetting

Live registration check, endorsements, conditions and notations reviewed.

National Police Check

Current (within 12 months) National Police Certificate sighted and filed.

Working Rights

Immunisation Status

Reference Checks

Minimum two clinical referees contacted and documented before placement.

Professional Indemnity

Insurance coverage confirmed. We also carry our own public liability and PI cover.

Clinical roles

Healthcare & support roles
we place

Regional and remote NSW facilities face healthcare and support challenges that metro services rarely encounter. We source workers specifically motivated for community-based care in these settings.

Residential Healthcare Worker

Cert III, Cert IV or equivalent qualification — personal care, mobility support, medication assistance, and daily living support in residential facilities. International qualifications are mapped to AQF Level 3 and gap training is organised prior to placement.

Community Healthcare Worker

Community-based workers providing in-home and outreach aged care support across dispersed regional populations.

AIN (Assistant in Nursing)

Rural and remote communities carry disproportionate mental health burden. Trained AINs supporting nursing and care teams in regional hospitals, multipurpose services, and community health settings.

Community Support Worker

Healthcare workers with cultural competency and experience working alongside community health workers and support teams across regional and remote NSW.

Dementia & Memory Care Worker

Workers with dementia care experience for regional aged care facilities where specialist dementia units are unavailable.

Disability Support Worker

Experienced support workers for individuals with disability and community care needs in regional communities — national police clearance, verified, and motivated for regional placement.

Palliative Care Support

Workers with palliative care awareness for multipurpose services and community settings where specialist palliative support is limited.

Aboriginal & Community Care Worker

Remote communities face high rates of diabetes, CVD and kidney disease. Workers with cultural awareness and experience supporting Aboriginal community members in aged care and disability services across regional NSW.
