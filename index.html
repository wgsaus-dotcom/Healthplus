International healthcare workforce

An international healthcare workforce
motivated to serve regional communities.

Many internationally trained healthcare workers actively seek the experience of regional Australian placements — drawn by purpose, community, and the opportunity to make a genuine difference. We identify them, credential them, and strategically deploy them to the communities that need them.

🇵🇭
Philippines
The Philippines produces some of the world's most compassionate and highly trained healthcare and support workers. Filipino care workers bring purpose-driven commitment, strong English communication, and a genuine motivation to serve regional communities. Many actively seek Australian placements as both a career and a life experience.
🇮🇳
India
India's aged care and support workforce is vast, deeply motivated, and culturally attuned to community-based care. Workers from India bring strong foundations in patient support, excellent English communication, and a genuine commitment to serving communities in need — an excellent fit for regional and remote NSW placements.
All international healthcare workers are credentialled and hold valid Australian working rights before any deployment offer is made to your facility or service.
1

Identified and assessed overseas

We source healthcare workers through partner associations and networks in each country — targeting those motivated by regional Australian placement.

2

Credentials verified

Qualifications assessed, reference checks and police clearances completed — working rights confirmed before any offer is made.

3

Working rights confirmed

VEVO check completed. We advise on appropriate visa pathways based on individual circumstances.

4

Deployed to your community

We are the employer. Your facility or service receives a fully credentialled, supported healthcare worker — all payroll and compliance managed by us.
