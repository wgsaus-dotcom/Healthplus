How this tool works

1

Enter the practitioner's details

Type the practitioner's name and select their profession. Adding the registration number makes the search more precise.

2

We open the AHPRA register

You're taken directly to the official AHPRA National Register of Practitioners with the search pre-filled — no copying and pasting.

3

Confirm their registration status

Check their registration is current, note any conditions or notations, verify the expiry date, and confirm their identity matches.

4

Document your check

Record the date and result of the verification. For placements, always re-verify immediately before each engagement — registration status can change.

What to look for on the AHPRA register

✓

Registration status: Registered

Must show "Registered" — not "Suspended", "Cancelled" or "No longer registered".

✓

Registration expiry date

Check the registration expiry date. Confirm it hasn't lapsed — some professions renew annually.

!

Conditions or notations

Any conditions on practice must be reviewed before placement. Some conditions restrict clinical settings or supervision requirements.

!

Endorsements

Check endorsements match the clinical role — e.g. Nurse Practitioner, Scheduled Medicines, or Midwifery endorsements.

✗

Name matches photo ID

AHPRA shows the registered legal name. Cross-check against the practitioner's government-issued photo ID before deployment.
