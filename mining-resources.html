INDUSTRY TERMINOLOGY

How we talk about what we do

The mining and resources sector uses precise, regulated language. We speak it fluently.

Labour Hire Provider

Health Plus International is a labour hire supplier — we employ the on-hired worker; the client site directs their daily tasks. This is the correct legal structure under the Labour Hire Licensing Act.

On-Hired Worker

The correct HR and legal term for the staff we place at a mine site or facility. They are employed by Health Plus International and on-hired to your operation under a Labour Hire Agreement.

Verification of Competency (VOC)

Confirms that a worker's skills are current and compliant with site safety standards. Required for specific equipment, tasks, and roles. We complete VOC verification before mobilisation.

Mobilisation

The end-to-end logistical process of deploying a worker to site — flights, accommodation, inductions, medicals, and site clearances. Managed entirely by Health Plus International.

FIFO / DIDO

Fly-In Fly-Out and Drive-In Drive-Out — the standard roster models for remote mining deployments. We coordinate both, including rotation scheduling and inter-site transfers.

Labour Hire Agreement

The formal commercial contract between Health Plus International and your operation. Establishes the terms under which on-hired workers are supplied, directed, and invoiced.
